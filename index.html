What This Certificate Does

This Root CA certificate establishes a chain of trust for all PDFs signed by the Antigravity PDF Signer app. When you verify a PDF:

  1. The verification script checks that the Digital ID matches the public key (prevents spoofing)
  2. It verifies that the user's certificate was signed by this Root CA (proves authenticity)
  3. If both checks pass, you can trust the PDF was signed by our genuine app
